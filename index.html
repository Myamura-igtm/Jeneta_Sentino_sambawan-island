Welcome to Sambawan Island

"Sambawan Island is one of the hidden gems of the Philippines, offering stunning white sand beaches and crystal-clear waters in a peaceful, untouched paradise."

Plan Your Visit
White sands. Crystal-clear waters. Sunset views like no other.

Rates and Fees

  • Entrance Fee – ₱120
  • Environmental Fee – ₱20
  • Beach Hut (8 pax) – ₱500/day
  • Lodging Cottage 1 (12–15 pax) – ₱2,500/day
  • Lodging Cottage 2 (2–6 pax) – ₱1,500/day
  • Tent Pitching – ₱100/day (small tent)

Activities

  • Snorkeling & Scuba Diving
  • Island Hopping
  • Camping & Overnight Stays
  • Trekking Adventures

How to Get There

  • From Tacloban City: Van or bus to Naval, Biliran (2–3 hours).
  • From Naval: Boat to Maripipi Island (1.5 hours).
  • From Maripipi: Small pump boat to Sambawan Island (15–20 minutes).

Travel Tips

  • Bring enough cash — limited ATMs available.
  • Pack eco-friendly essentials like reef-safe sunscreen and reusable water bottles.
  • Check the weather forecast for the best experience.
